Culture During the Gupta Empire

Art and Architecture

The Gupta period saw the development of the Nagara style of temple architecture. The caves of Ajanta and Ellora showcase the pinnacle of Indian rock-cut architecture and painting.

Literature

Sanskrit literature flourished with works by Kalidasa, including plays like Shakuntala and poems like Meghaduta. The Puranas were also compiled during this period.

Science and Mathematics

Aryabhata's work on astronomy and mathematics, including the concept of zero and the approximation of pi, were groundbreaking. Varahamihira made significant contributions to astronomy.
