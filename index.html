Embark on a Journey Through Time

Discover the rich tapestry of Indian history, from ancient civilizations to modern marvels

Begin Your Adventure

Indus Valley Civilization

3300 BCE - 1300 BCE

One of the world's earliest urban civilizations, known for its advanced city planning and drainage systems.

Maurya Empire

322 BCE - 185 BCE

India's first empire, reaching its peak under Emperor Ashoka, known for spreading Buddhism.

Gupta Empire

320 CE - 550 CE

Often referred to as the "Golden Age" of Indian history, with significant developments in science and arts.

Mughal Empire

1526 CE - 1857 CE

Known for its cultural and architectural achievements, including the Taj Mahal.

Maratha Empire

1674 CE - 1818 CE

A Hindu confederacy that rose to prominence in the 18th century, challenging Mughal dominance.

British Colonial Period

1858 CE - 1947 CE

The period of direct British rule in India, marked by significant social and economic changes.

Post Independence India

1947 CE - Present

Modern India as a sovereign, democratic republic, emerging as a global power.
