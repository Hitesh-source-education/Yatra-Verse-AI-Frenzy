Mauryan Culture

Arts and Architecture

The Mauryan period saw a flourishing of art and architecture, with the development of the distinctive Mauryan polish on stone sculptures and the construction of monumental structures like the Great Stupa at Sanchi and the Pillars of Ashoka.

Religion and Philosophy

Under Ashoka, Buddhism gained royal patronage and spread across Asia. The empire also saw the coexistence of various other religions and philosophies, including Hinduism and Jainism.

Science and Technology

The Mauryan era witnessed advancements in metallurgy, especially iron-working. The period also saw developments in astronomy, mathematics, and medicine.
