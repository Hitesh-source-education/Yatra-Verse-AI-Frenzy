Timeline of the Indus Valley Civilization

3300 BCE

Early Harappan Phase begins, marking the start of the Indus Valley Civilization.

2600 BCE

Mature Harappan Phase - Urban centers flourish with advanced infrastructure.

1900 BCE

Late Harappan Phase begins, showing signs of cultural changes and urban decline.

1300 BCE

The civilization gradually disappears, leaving behind a rich archaeological legacy.
