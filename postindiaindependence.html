Progress Since Independence

Education

Literacy rate increased from 18% in 1951 to over 74% in 2021.

Economy

GDP growth from $37 billion in 1947 to over $2.6 trillion in 2020.

Life Expectancy

Increased from 32 years in 1947 to 69 years in 2020.

Nuclear Power

India developed Asia's first nuclear reactor, the Apsara, in 1956.

Digital Payments

In 2022, India was one of five countries to top the list of digital payments.

Healthcare

People have access to free treatments at government hospitals
